# Weightless Daily Tracker - User Guide

Welcome to the **Weightless Daily Tracker**, a futuristic and frictionless way to manage your daily tasks, track your focus time, and build momentum.

## Getting Started

1.  **Open the App**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2.  **Navigate Dates**: Use the `←` and `→` arrows at the top to view your history or plan for tomorrow. Your data automatically saves to your browser!

## Adding Tasks
At the top of the tracker:
1. Type your task name in the input box (e.g., "Deep Work").
2. Type the estimated time in minutes in the `Mins` box (e.g., "45").
3. Click **Add** or press `Enter`. 
*Note: You must set an accurate time from the start, as you won't be able to easily change it later without passing a focus test!*

## The Timer System
*   **Start**: Click "Start" on any task to begin the countdown.
*   **Live Countdown**: The app locks your task and begins a live digital countdown.

### The 10-Minute Crunch
To prevent you from casually pausing your work, the **Pause** and **+5m (Extend)** buttons will remain invisible until you are in the final 10 minutes of your task. 

### Math Verification
If you try to Pause or Extend your time, a security modal will pop up with a simple addition problem (e.g., `What is 6 + 3?`). You must type the correct answer to prove you are making a conscious decision, rather than clicking impulsively.

## Completion & Statistics
A task will automatically mark itself as **Completed** when the timer hits `0:00`.
Alternatively, you can click the "In Progress" button to **Complete Early**.

Once completed, the tracker analyzes your performance against your original estimate and awards you a badge:
*   🟢 **Saved: Xm**: You finished the task faster than expected!
*   🔴 **Extra: Xm**: You had to extend the timer to finish.
*   🔵 **On Time**: You finished exactly when the timer hit zero.

## Exporting Your Day
Want to share your daily progress? Click the **Camera Icon (📷)** in the top left corner. The app will hide all buttons, freeze the floating animation, and download a beautiful PNG screenshot of your daily log!
